Portfolio Website Proposal

Objective

A comprehensive view of my life journey that includes my professional, academic, spontaneous, international, and personal endeavors. This will act as a portfolio to showcase my growth, achievements, and experiences across these facets, aimed at connecting on a personal level with potential employers, collaborators, and peers.

Target Audience

The target audience includes prospective employers, graduate admissions committees, professional peers, individuals interested in my work and contributions to the community, and anyone who wants to get to know me.

Content Plan

The website will consist of the following pages:

  • Home: A welcoming page introducing the portfolio and its purpose.
  • About Me: A detailed biography covering my background, interests, and values.
  • Professional Work: A showcase of my professional experiences, including roles, responsibilities, and achievements.
  • Academic Work: Highlights of academic achievements, including coursework, research, and extracurricular activities.
  • Projects: A gallery of selected projects, including descriptions, objectives, and outcomes.
  • Travel: Insights into my travel experiences, highlighting cultural exposure and personal growth.
  • Community Service: A section dedicated to my volunteer work and initiatives contributing to societal well-being.
  • Contact: A form and links for visitors to connect with me.
  • Sitemap: A page summarizing the website structure for easy navigation.
  • Credits: Acknowledgments and references for external resources used on the site.

Website Structure

The structure of the website will demonstrate both breadth and depth:

  • Home
  • About Me
  • Professional Work
  • Academic Work
    • i.Academic Journey
    • ii.Research and Coursework
    • iii.Achievements and Awards
  • Projects
    • i.Professional Projects
    • ii.Personal Projects
  • Travel
    • i. Cultural Experiences
    • ii.Personal Growth
  • Community Service
  • Contact
  • Sitemap
  • Credits
