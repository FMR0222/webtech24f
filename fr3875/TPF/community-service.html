Community Service Journey

Empowering communities through social impact

Inspired by Family Values

Growing up, giving back was a natural part of life. My parents’ dedication to community work set the foundation for my own involvement. From volunteering in local charity events to supporting my mothers community programs, I learned the importance of making a positive impact early on.

School and Community Projects

As I got older, my passion for community service grew. I became more involved in school initiatives like refurbishing underfunded schools and organizing local charity events. These experiences helped me realize how small actions can lead to meaningful change.

Babson's Emphasis on Social Impact

Babson’s commitment to social entrepreneurship is a big reason I chose it. Being part of the Natalie Taylor Scholar Program has allowed me to continue my community service efforts while learning how to make a larger impact through entrepreneurship.
